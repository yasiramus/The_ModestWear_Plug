    History

    People have loved clothing fashion for thousands of years. From the early days to the day clothes have become the expression of who we are. Throughout history, drawings, documents and other archaeological finds have also revealed fashion worn by people in various ancient civilizations. The Greeks were wearing clothes in different styles from their eastern neighbors. Both women and men were usually wearing thick woolen long dresses. Fashion designing is a powerful industry, spread all over the world. The term itself can be defined as creating appealing apparel, but also has been widened to accessory design.
